Help

This website shows the most recent votes taken by the United Nations General Assembly.


The 'Latest Votes' tab shows the most recent votes, with a brief description of the resolution and a bar chart showing the voting results, unless the resolution was passed without a vote. In the UN, member states can vote to accept, reject, or abstain from a resolution. If a member state does not vote, this is also recorded. Resolutions are passed if they receive a majority of 'yes' votes. The four most recent votes are shown, with the option to view more.

The votes of each member state for each resolution, where available, can be viewed by clicking on the down arrow symbol to the right of the bar chart.


The 'Member States' tab shows a summary of votes for each member state on all recent resolutions where voting was required.


The site is updated regularly to reflect the most recent resolutions passed by the General Assembly; data are taken from the United Nations Digital Library. Descriptions for each resolution are generated by AI, so may not reflect the contents of each resolution with complete accuracy.

Cooperation between the United Nations and the Community of Portuguese-speaking Countries Jan 28

Strengthens cooperation between the United Nations and the Community of Portuguese-speaking Countries (CPLP) to promote peace, security, sustainable development, and human rights within the CPLP's member states and across its areas of operation.

This resolution was passed without a vote

Promotion of durable peace through sustainable development in Africa Jan 15

Promotes the achievement of durable peace and sustainable development in Africa by emphasizing inclusive and equitable economic growth, strengthening governance, and addressing the root causes of conflict.

This resolution was passed without a vote

New Partnership for Africa's Development : progress in implementation and international support Jan 15

Acknowledges the progress made in implementing the New Partnership for Africa's Development (NEPAD) while highlighting persistent challenges and urging continued international support for its sustainable development goals.

This resolution was passed without a vote

Promotion of international cooperation to combat illicit financial flows and strengthen good practices on assets return to foster sustainable development Dec 24

Promotes international cooperation to combat illicit financial flows, urging states to strengthen asset recovery mechanisms and return stolen assets to their countries of origin to support sustainable development.

This resolution was passed without a vote

Financial reports and audited financial statements, and reports of the Board of Auditors Dec 24

The UN General Assembly resolution approves the financial reports and audited financial statements of the UN for the fiscal year 2022, and endorses the Board of Auditors' reports.

This resolution was passed without a vote

Strengthening International Cooperation for Combating Certain Crimes Committed by Means of Information and Communications Technology Systems and for the Sharing of Evidence in Electronic Form of Serious Crimes Dec 24

Promotes the strengthening of international cooperation to combat crimes committed using information and communications technology, and facilitates the cross-border sharing of electronic evidence for serious crimes.

This resolution was passed without a vote
